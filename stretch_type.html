Stretch Marks

Hi There, "Stretch Marks" is an experiment that invites you to explore the materiality of digitally typed texts and their gradual degradation. On it, the letters are stretched thus distorted according to the duration of the key press. Additionally, as the code uses the previous iteration of that letter as the new source, each repetition of a key causes the letter to increasingly lose resolution and clarity.

To try it out, try typing on your keyboard.
