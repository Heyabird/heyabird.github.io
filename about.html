Hey! My name is Heya (해야).

I'm a programmer, musician, and artist from Seoul and currently based in Oakland, California.

During the day, I work for Bandcamp as a software engineer with the mission to help independent musicians earn more.

After work, I like to explore the topics of Buddhism, mutual support, anthropology, society, technology, and labor. I also enjoy taking meditation walks, creating art, and making music.

Most recently, I've been interested in making computational and physical experiments that encourage contemplation and convey a sense of humor and care.
