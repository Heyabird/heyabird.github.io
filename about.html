Heya, World!

My name is Heya 해나 Kwon. I am a Korean American artist, designer, and researcher working across sound, new media, and participatory practices. Originally from Seoul, Korea, I worked for several years in different parts of California. I currently live and go to school in Espoo, Finland.

I enjoy working on art and design that involve participatory methods. My research practice examines the psychological, material, and ecological conditions of media systems with a focus on the relationship between bodies and media. A question I've been asking are: how can we live with slowness, playfulness, and care in our current extractive and accelerationist technological regimes? How can art and technology help in figuring that out?

Before going back to school, I worked as a software engineer at Bandcamp with a mission to give musicians eqitable pay. Before that, I lived as an artist while working in the service industry.

On my free time, I like to draw, vlog, walk, and journal.
